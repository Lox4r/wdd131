SoleMate Shoes - Website Plan

Site Name

SoleMate Shoes

This name means "perfect shoe companion," clearly showing we sell shoes. It's easy to remember.

Example Domain: solemateshoes.com

Site Purpose

Our website will be an online store for shoes. We'll:

  • Show a variety of shoes for all people.
  • Give details and pictures for each shoe.
  • Make buying shoes online easy and safe.
  • Offer good customer support and clear return policies.

Scenarios (Visitor Questions)

Here are questions someone visiting our site might ask:

  1. "What running shoes are best for long runs, especially if I have high arches?"
  2. "Do you have shoes made without animal products (vegan), and how can I find them?"
  3. "If my shoes don't fit, can I return them, and how does that work?"

Color Choices

Our site will use warm, friendly colors. We've picked:

  • Main Color: #A0522D (Sienna Brown)
    • Used for: Headings, buttons, and important links. It feels warm and strong.
  • Secondary Color: #D2B48C (Tan)
    • Used for: Borders, smaller titles, and background highlights. It adds a natural touch.
  • Background Color: #F8F8F8 (Light Gray)
    • Used for: The main background of the page. It's clean and helps content stand out.
  • Text Color: #36454F (Charcoal Gray)
    • Used for: Most of the text. It's easy to read against the light background.

Fonts Used

We chose simple, modern fonts that are easy to read:

  • For Titles: Montserrat
    • Used for: All main headings (like "Site Name," "Color Choices"). It's clean and modern.
  • For Regular Text: Open Sans
    • Used for: All paragraphs, product descriptions, and general information. It's very clear and easy to read.
